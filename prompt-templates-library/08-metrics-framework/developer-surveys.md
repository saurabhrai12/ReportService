## 5. Survey Analysis Template

### Scoring Guide

| Category | Questions | Calculation | Benchmark |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Productivity Score | Q1-6 (baseline), Q4-6 (monthly) | Average of ratings | ≥7.0 |
| Adoption Rate | Q1 (monthly) | % daily+ users | ≥70% |
| Time Saved | Q6 (monthly) | Average hours | ≥5 hrs/week |
| Quality Impact | Q7-9 (monthly) | Composite score | ≥7.0 |
| NPS | Q14 (monthly) | % Promoters - % Detractors | ≥30 |
